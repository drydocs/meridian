The problem

Inflation is winning.
Your savings are losing.

Across West Africa, inflation runs double-digit while savings accounts pay a fraction of that. Most DeFi makes it worse — not better.

Nigeria's average inflation in 2024, per the National Bureau of Statistics. Every naira saved in a bank loses a third of its purchasing power in a year.

The average fee to receive a remittance in West Africa, per the World Bank 2023 report. Banks extract hundreds of millions in transfer fees every year.

The cost to move any amount of USDC on Stellar, settling in 5 seconds. Not $50 in Ethereum gas. A fraction of a cent — designed for real people.

How it works

Three clicks to earning yield.

01

Connect your Freighter wallet

Freighter is a free Stellar browser extension. No seed phrase sharing, no custodial risk. You hold your keys — always.

02

See live APY across all vaults

Meridian pulls real-time rates from Blend Capital and DeFindex. Every vault ranked by yield. No spreadsheets, no guesswork.

03

Deposit in one click

Approve a single transaction in Freighter. Meridian builds the unsigned XDR — your private key never leaves your device.
